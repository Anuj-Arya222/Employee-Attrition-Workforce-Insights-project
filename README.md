# Employee-Attrition-Workforce-Insights-project
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition to identify key factors influencing employee turnover and provide actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To understand why employees leave the organization and identify high-risk groups using data analysis and visualization.

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Employee dataset with 200,000+ records
* Features include:

  * Age, Gender, Department, Job Role
  * Monthly Income, Years at Company
  * Job Satisfaction, Work-Life Balance
  * Overtime, Attrition

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* Power BI
* Data Analysis & Visualization

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

* Converted categorical values (Yes/No → 1/0)
* Created new features (Income Groups, Risk Segments)
* Cleaned and structured dataset

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

* Attrition Rate Calculation
* Attrition by Overtime
* Attrition by Job Satisfaction
* Department-wise Attrition Analysis
* Combined analysis (Overtime + Satisfaction)

---

## 🔥 Key Insights

* Overtime increases attrition by more than 2x
* Employees with low job satisfaction show significantly higher churn
* Even satisfied employees leave under high workload
* Overtime + low satisfaction is the highest risk group

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards:

  * Total Employees
  * Attrition Rate
  * Active Employees
  * Average Salary

* Visualizations:

  * Attrition by Overtime
  * Attrition by Job Satisfaction
  * Combined Overtime & Satisfaction Analysis

* Interactive Filters:

  * Department
  * Gender
  * Job Role

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime
* Improve employee satisfaction
* Monitor high-risk employee segments
* Promote work-life balance policies

---

## 🏆 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can uncover critical workforce insights and support better HR decision-making.
